● Planning sessions sit at the end of each period — planning ahead for the next three periods.

Rolling 3 periods – what applies at each planning session
Period 1 – Locked Next period

The plan is set. Execute as-is — no changes are made.

Period 2 – Review The period after

Reviewed at each session. Can be adjusted based on new insights or shifting priorities.

Period 3 – Draft Further ahead

Rough directional plan. Fully open to change — a statement of intent.

At each planning session: Period 1 is locked, Period 2 is reviewed and adjusted, Period 3 is updated with new direction. Then everything rolls one step forward.

Input ahead of each planning session
Pipeline & insights from sales What's driving deals right now? Which segments are moving?
Top-performing channels What worked last period? What should we do more of?
Competitor check What's changed in the market? Any new positioning moves?
Events & trade shows Which dates drive timing for upcoming periods?
Always on – not planned per period
